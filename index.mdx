If you're setting up for the first time, complete [brand onboarding](/onboarding/brand-onboarding) before using Emma. Emma uses your brand profile, founder voice, ICP, and Knowledge Base as context for everything she generates.

## What Emma does inside the portal

- Maintains brand guidelines, founder profiles, ICP definitions, and competitor analysis.
- Drafts blog posts, LinkedIn content, ad copy, newsletters, and landing page text.
- Creates and manages Meta and LinkedIn ad campaigns. Reports on performance.
- Manages CRM lists. Enriches leads with web search. Scores leads against ICP. Drafts personalized outreach.
- Generates landing pages and deploys them.
- Aggregates performance data across paid social, newsletter, and SEO.
