## What Emma can do inside the portal

- Build and update brand guidelines, founder profiles, ICP definitions, and competitor analysis
- Write blog posts, LinkedIn content, ad copy, newsletters, and landing page text
- Create and run Meta and LinkedIn ad campaigns, then report on performance
- Manage CRM lists, enrich leads, score them against your ICP, and draft personalized outreach
- Generate landing pages and push them live
- Pull performance data across paid social, newsletter, and SEO
