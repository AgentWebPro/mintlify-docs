**Emma by AgentWeb** is the AI marketing operating system for lean B2B marketing teams. Most teams are now told to "go AI" but end up with a sprawl of disconnected tools and generic output no marketer would ship. Emma learns a company's brand, ICP, and positioning, then runs the real marketing work: research, prospecting, on-brand ads and outbound, and landing pages deployed to the customer's own domain. Emma is vetted against real marketing best practices and sharpened every week on actual pipeline she generates. Customers like xCures run their entire marketing motion on Emma instead of stitching tools together or handing their brand to an agency.

This portal is your command center — create and manage tasks, use templates, and chat with **Emma**, your AI marketing assistant.

## What Emma does inside the portal

- Maintains brand guidelines, founder profiles, ICP definitions, and competitor analysis.
- Drafts blog posts, LinkedIn content, ad copy, newsletters, and landing page text.
- Creates and manages Meta and LinkedIn ad campaigns. Reports on performance.
- Manages CRM lists. Enriches leads with web search. Scores leads against ICP. Drafts personalized outreach.
- Generates landing pages and deploys them.
- Aggregates performance data across paid social, newsletter, and SEO.
