AgentWeb offers two complementary Google Drive integrations:

| Integration | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Connected Account** | Emma creates and edits Google Docs, Sheets, and folders directly in your Drive from chat. |
| **Knowledge Base** | Emma reads files from folders you share with AgentWeb, as a Knowledge Base source. |

---

## Connected Account

Connect your Google account so Emma can create and manage files in your Drive on your behalf.

### Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Accounts">
    In the portal, go to **Settings → Accounts** and find the Google Drive card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    AgentWeb redirects you to Google's sign-in screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in and authorize">
    Sign in with the Google account whose Drive you want Emma to use. Review the requested permissions and click **Allow**.

    Google will ask for permission to create and manage files in your Drive, access your Google Docs and Sheets, and view your basic profile info. Emma only touches files she creates — she won't read, modify, or delete anything else in your Drive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm in AgentWeb">
    You're returned to the Accounts tab and the Google Drive card flips to **Connected**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What Emma can do

| Capability | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **Create Google Doc** | Creates a new Google Doc in your Drive. Emma confirms the title and asks where to put it before creating. |
| **Create Google Sheet** | Creates a new Google Sheet. Same confirmation and location prompts as Docs. |
| **Create folder** | Creates a new folder in your Drive or inside an existing folder. |
| **Update Google Doc** | Edits a Google Doc Emma created — including ones from previous sessions. |
| **Update Google Sheet** | Writes or updates data in a Google Sheet Emma created — including ones from previous sessions. |
| **List folder contents** | Lists the files inside a folder Emma created. |

<Info>
Emma always confirms before creating or editing a file. If you ask her to "create a doc" without specifying a title or location, she'll ask for both before proceeding.
</Info>

### What Emma can and can't access

Emma can only work with files and folders she created through AgentWeb. She cannot read or modify files you created yourself or files shared with you by others.

Files Emma creates are owned by your Google account and appear in your Drive like any other file. Emma can reference files she created in previous sessions as long as they haven't been deleted.

<Note>
To let Emma read files you didn't create through AgentWeb — such as existing Docs or files shared by teammates — use the [Knowledge Base integration](#knowledge-base) below.
</Note>

### Example prompts

- *"Create a Google Doc called Q3 Campaign Brief and put it in my Marketing folder."*
- *"Make a new sheet to track our outreach pipeline for this quarter."*
- *"Update the Q3 Campaign Brief with the section headings we just discussed."*
- *"Create a folder called Client Reports inside my Projects folder."*
- *"What files are in the Client Reports folder?"*

### Disconnect

Click the Google Drive card in **Settings → Accounts** and choose **Disconnect**. You can also revoke access at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions). Disconnecting does not delete any files Emma created — they remain in your Drive.

## Knowledge Base

Share Google Drive folders with AgentWeb so Emma can search and read them as a Knowledge Base source, alongside uploaded files and ClickUp Docs. This is how you give Emma access to files you already have in your Drive — documents you wrote, spreadsheets shared by your team, and so on.

AgentWeb has read-only access and can only see the folders you share.

### Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Copy AgentWeb's sharing email">
    In the portal, go to **Brand → Knowledge Base** and find the Google Drive section. Copy the email address shown there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share a folder with AgentWeb">
    In Google Drive, right-click the folder you want to share, click **Share**, paste the email address you copied, set permission to **Viewer**, and click **Send**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the folder in AgentWeb">
    Back in AgentWeb's Google Drive settings, click **Add Folder** and paste the folder ID.

    To find the folder ID, open the folder in Google Drive. The URL looks like `https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1a2B3c4D5e`. The ID is the string after `/folders/` — in this example, `1a2B3c4D5e`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify access">
    AgentWeb verifies access and displays the folder name. Files are now available to Emma.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can add multiple folders. To remove one, click the delete icon next to it.

### What Emma can do

| Capability | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **Search** | Searches your shared folders by file content. Results include the folder and file name as the source. |
| **Browse** | Lists all files across your connected folders (up to 100 files per folder). |
| **Read** | Reads the full content of an individual file, including Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, PDFs, and plain text files. |

### Supported file types

| File type | How it's read |
|---|---|
| Google Docs | Exported as plain text. |
| Google Sheets | Exported as CSV. |
| Google Slides | Exported as plain text. |
| PDF | Text extracted automatically. |
| Text files (.txt, .md, .csv, .json, .xml, .html) | Read directly. |

Other file types are read as binary data. Very large files are truncated at 50,000 characters.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Google Drive card doesn't appear in Settings → Accounts">
    The Google Drive connected account feature may not be enabled on your plan. Contact your admin or reach out to [support@agentweb.pro](mailto:support@agentweb.pro).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Emma says she can't read a file I created">
    Emma can only access files she created through AgentWeb. For files you created yourself or that were shared with you, use the [Knowledge Base integration](#knowledge-base) — share the folder containing those files with AgentWeb.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Emma created a file but I can't find it in Drive">
    Files Emma creates appear in **My Drive** root or in the folder you specified. You can also search Google Drive by file name.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Access denied when adding a Knowledge Base folder">
    Confirm the folder is shared with the AgentWeb email address shown in **Brand → Knowledge Base**. Sharing settings in Google Drive sometimes take a minute to propagate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Emma sees a Knowledge Base folder but can't read a specific file">
    The file may be in a sub-folder that wasn't shared. Sharing a parent folder shares everything inside it, but new sub-folders added later sometimes need to be re-shared explicitly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Large file appears truncated">
    Files over 50,000 characters are truncated to keep responses fast. Split very long documents into multiple files or summarize them first.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
