LinkedIn has three separate connections in AgentWeb, each for a different capability. Connect whichever ones you need.

| Connection | What it enables |
|---|---|
| **LinkedIn** (organic) | Publish posts to your profile or company page. Pull engagement metrics. |
| **LinkedIn Outreach** | Send connection requests and personalized DMs at scale. |
| **LinkedIn Ads** | Manage LinkedIn ad accounts, campaigns, and creatives. |

You can have all three connected simultaneously.

## Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Accounts">
    In the portal, go to **Settings → Accounts** and find the LinkedIn card for the capability you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    AgentWeb redirects you to LinkedIn's OAuth flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the account or page">
    For LinkedIn (organic), pick the personal profile or company page to connect.

    For LinkedIn Ads, pick the ad account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The card flips to **Connected**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Emma can do

### LinkedIn (organic)

- Publish posts (text, image, carousel, document) to your profile or company page.
- Schedule posts for later.
- Comment on posts from your account.
- React (like, celebrate, etc.) on posts from your account.
- Pull per-post engagement metrics into [Performance → Paid Social](/user-guide/performance#paid-social).

### LinkedIn Outreach

- Send connection requests with personalized messages.
- Send DMs to existing connections.
- Run sequences against a [CRM lead list](/user-guide/crm) — connection request, then follow-up DM, etc.
- Respects LinkedIn's rate limits to avoid account flags.

### LinkedIn Ads

- List ad accounts, campaigns, and ad groups.
- Pull campaign insights (impressions, clicks, conversions, spend).
- Read ad creatives and audience targeting.

## Permissions

LinkedIn (organic) and LinkedIn Ads use LinkedIn's standard OAuth scopes for posting and reading analytics.

LinkedIn Outreach uses Unipile under the hood, which connects via your LinkedIn session. Treat your Outreach connection like an active LinkedIn login — if you sign out everywhere on LinkedIn, you'll need to reconnect Outreach.

## Disconnect

Click the LinkedIn card in **Settings → Accounts** and choose **Disconnect** for the capability you want to remove. Disconnecting one capability doesn't affect the others.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connected but posts don't appear">
    Confirm you connected the right account or page. If you connected a personal profile but expected posts on a company page, disconnect and reconnect with the company page selected.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="LinkedIn Outreach connection drops repeatedly">
    Outreach piggybacks on your LinkedIn session. Multiple device logins, security challenges, and 2FA prompts can invalidate the session. Reconnect from Settings → Accounts and complete any pending security challenges in LinkedIn directly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ad account doesn't appear in campaign lists">
    AgentWeb caches the list of ad accounts at connection time. Disconnect LinkedIn Ads and reconnect to refresh.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
